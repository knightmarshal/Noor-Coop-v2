Vision & Mission

To help SME's
meet their business goals and objectives.

SME's are growing at a rapid pace. The problem is that the financing required to expand the business or to invest in new innovative technologies remains scarce.

Noor-Coop is an SME focused platform that brings these three worlds together: project beneficiaries raise projects while financiers are able to invest in projects. Noor Coop is the first peer-to-peer collaborating platform for SMEs and financiers.

Noor Coop offers a secure and transparent way to raise funds through project presentation of skilled beneficiaries. Financiers can invest in specific projects to get a return on their investments at set period.

How it works?

Don't wait.
The time will never be just right, join the Coop today.

Sign up to be a member of the Coop either as a borrower or financier.
After a successful registration, then you are able to access projects and finance them or raise projects and get financed by verified financiers.

As a Noor-Coop Member, you are able to raise projects for financing and also able to earn while you finance other people's projects.

To become a Noor-Coop Member, you will have to register with us and pay the compulsory registration fee of ₦1,000.

Noor-Coop does not make profit, we only share returns on investment.
